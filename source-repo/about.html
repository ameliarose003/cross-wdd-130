About the Author

Hello! My name is Mari Cross, author of the Warriors of Shoresa series. I have been writing since I was in the 5th grade. Though I was never fond of writing growing up, I had plenty of practice with storytelling. Every night, I would tell my siblings stories with adventure, castles, and magic. I take pleasure in using my gift of writing so many people can enjoy the stories and adventures swirling around in my head. I look forward to seeing where the these stories take me, for I am as invested in the stories as anyone else. A little bit about my personal life: I am the oldest of 7, I am currently attending college as an English Major, and I love pandas.
